Emotet is a sophisticated malware strain and cybercrime operation, first detected in 2014. Initially a banking Trojan, it evolved into a highly modular loader and botnet, delivering other malware like TrickBot, QBot, Ryuk, and IcedID, and often used in ransomware campaigns.
